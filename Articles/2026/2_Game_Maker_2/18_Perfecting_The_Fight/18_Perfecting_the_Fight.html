~18 Perfecting the Fight~

By StarsInDust

5/11/2026

 

 

In a previous tutorial, we created our fight room, and we threw our characters into this room, but are they truly performing in the manner that we would expect them to? They seem to be sizing each other up, and looking at each other menacingly, but when it comes to this fight, do we really have any true interaction between the two.

After all, this insane fight is taking place with our characters clear across the room from one another. No connecting blows, no flying sparks of magic, NOTHING. What? Are these two fighting telepathically? Sure we have the score board progression bar moving, but visually we can’t understand what it is measuring. I think this room needs a bit of help, and in this tutorial, we are going to enhance the code, so that it gets it.

Fixing Errors

If the two entities seem to be moving through each other, instead of hitting each other and then hitting open air. We will need to adjust the lunge_stop_distance in the obj_hero_fight CREATE event. If the hero is moving through to the backside of the slime you would think that you need to decrease the stop distance, but actually reducing the space he moves here is an increase of the lunge stop distance.

Changing the lunge_stop_distance on the Slime

Just increasing this setting for the hero, is not enough. Especially if you find your enemy object is also moving through the hero. He has his own lunge_stop_distance, and you will need to change the code in here too, if you want things to look right.

 

Working with the 3 files that we have here, seems to do a pretty good job of enhancing the movements of these characters. They are not using any flying magic or any other projectiles, but beginning with this simple lunge manuver, is a great starting point. So, I will end this tutorial here.
