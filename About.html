~About Us~

This may not exactly be like many of the About Us pages that are typically out there today. But maybe at this stage of my life, and as a grandmother, the best thing that I can impart on anyone out there is a bit of wisdom. I have probably learned more than anyone else, but bumping my head and figuring out “Nope, that isn’t the way I should be going” so if I can prevent a few injured head bumps and a bit of bruised egos, then just maybe some of the things that I try to do in this life may actually turn out to be worthwhile.

  • Decisions have consequence, think before you leap
  • Exercise your mind as well as your body every day
  • Eat as your grandparents ate, none of this processed or fast food. Keep it Real
  • Oh, and then there are the things I listed below. A few things to cherish and abide by
  • Ambition is a Goal
  • Time to Care
  • Build Trust, Resolve Issues
  • Let's Learn
  • Spend it with the ones we Love
  • Integrety is a Grand Quality
